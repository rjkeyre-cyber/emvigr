About Emvigr

Our Mission

We create immersive VR experiences that safeguard at-risk students by combining engagement and educational support in safe virtual environments.

Our Vision

Empowering schools with technology that nurtures social, emotional, and cognitive skills through gamified VR experiences.

Our Values

Safety, inclusivity, innovation, and measurable impact guide everything we create for students and educators.

Who We Help

At-Risk Students

Students who face social, emotional, or learning challenges can explore safe VR scenarios designed to build resilience and confidence.

Educators

Teachers and school staff gain tools to monitor engagement, improve classroom inclusion, and integrate VR safely into learning.

Schools

Institutions receive measurable impact insights and solutions to improve student wellbeing, attendance, and engagement.

Our Games

Resilience Quest

Adventure-based VR scenarios that help students manage stress and build resilience in challenging situations.

Empathy Explorer

Interactive social simulations that foster empathy, perspective-taking, and collaborative problem-solving skills.

Focus VR

Mindfulness and attention-training games designed to improve concentration and emotional regulation for students.
