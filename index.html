About Emvigr

Emvigr creates immersive VR experiences designed to safeguard at-risk students. We combine engagement, educational support, and safe virtual environments to help students build resilience, confidence, and social-emotional skills. Our mission is to empower schools and educators with tools that promote wellbeing, inclusion, and measurable impact.

Who We Help

We work with schools, educators, and support staff to identify students at risk of social, emotional, or academic difficulties, providing VR experiences tailored to build confidence and resilience in a safe, controlled environment.

Our Games

Our VR games simulate real-world scenarios in a safe space, allowing students to practice social-emotional skills, decision-making, and coping strategies. Each experience is evidence-based and designed with education experts.
